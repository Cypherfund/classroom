Two factor Authentication (BETA)

Two Factor Authentication adds an additional layer of security to your Coursera account. Each time you log in to the Platform, you will be asked to enter a unique code that is only available on your mobile phone. This extra protection ensures that you are the only one who will have access to your Course account and courses.
